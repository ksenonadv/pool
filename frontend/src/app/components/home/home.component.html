Cue Frenzy
Game Rules
  • The game is played with 15 object balls and a cue ball.
  • Players are assigned either solids (1-7) or stripes (9-15) after the break.
  • Pocket all your group of balls, then legally pocket the 8-ball to win.
  • Fouls include scratching the cue ball or failing to hit your own group first.
How to Play
  1. Click "Play Now" to start a new game.
  2. Use your mouse or finger to aim and shoot.
  3. Try to pocket your assigned balls before your opponent.
  4. Pocket the 8-ball last to win!
